Lima is the capital and largest city of Peru, located on the central western coast of South America along the Pacific Ocean. It is home to nearly a third of the country's population and serves as the political, cultural, and economic heart of Peru. Lima is best known for its rich colonial architecture, world-renowned cuisine, and its unique blend of ancient Incan heritage with modern urban life.
